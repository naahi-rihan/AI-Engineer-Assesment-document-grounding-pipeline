CASE BACKGROUND

Client: Wyatt Industries, Inc. (Plaintiff)
Opposing Party: Norwood Consulting Group, LLC (Defendant)

Wyatt Industries engaged Norwood Consulting Group under a Master Services Agreement 
(MSA) dated January 15, 2022. The contract was valued at $4,200,000 over a 36-month 
engagement period. Services included strategic advisory, operational restructuring, and 
technology implementation support.

KEY CONTRACT PROVISIONS

Section 4.2 - Performance Standards:
Consultant shall maintain a client satisfaction score of no less than 80% as measured 
by quarterly reviews. Failure to meet this standard for two consecutive quarters shall 
constitute a material breach.

Section 7.1 - Termination for Cause:
Client may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if Consultant 
commits a material breach and fails to cure such breach within 30 days of receiving 
notice.

Section 9.3 - Liability Cap:
In no event shall either party's aggregate liability exceed the total fees paid in 
the six months preceding the claim.
