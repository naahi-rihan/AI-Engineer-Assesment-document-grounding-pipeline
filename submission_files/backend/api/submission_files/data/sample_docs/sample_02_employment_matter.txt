MATTER SUMMARY

Client: Dr. Linda Chen (Plaintiff / Claimant)
Opposing Party: Brightside Financial Partners LP

Dr. Chen was employed as Managing Director of Research Analytics at Brightside 
Financial Partners from March 2019 to February 2024. She was terminated on 
February 28, 2024, with 4 weeks severance pay.

Dr. Chen alleges: (1) wrongful termination in retaliation for internal whistleblower 
complaint; (2) gender discrimination under Title VII and NY HRL; (3) breach of 
implied employment contract.
